Responsibility

Alongside our commercial and financial responsibilities, we recognise our wider responsibilities to society.

To make sure we can deliver our commitments to the patients, stakeholders and shareholders who rely on us, we are focused on improving the way we do business. Whether that is operating with transparency in everything we do, and listening to the views of all of the people involved in healthcare decisions.

To ensure we do this, our business operates to the Pfizer Code of Conduct, as well as the regulations that govern the pharmaceutical industry globally and in the UK.
